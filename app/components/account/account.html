Information and Registering as a Player

You currently are a standard user for CompeteLeague! If you're new to the site and want to learn more you can check out our ABOUT US page for information! We run League of Legends leagues in an LCS format for players at all levels. If you'd like to sign up as a player and start competing then you can register by hitting the button below and filling out a form.

Information and being a Free Agent

You're currently a Free Agent! You're listed on our free agent database under the summoner name X. You can view the free agent database to find other free agents and form a team! To view other free agents go to the database via the button below, filter for a player via rank and click their Display Name to see their Profile page. From here you can see their details, and if you want to form a team with them you can get in touch by leaving a comment below their profile or email them via the "message" button. You can edit your own Player Profile by following the "My Player Profile Page" link above.

Create a Team

When you've formed a roster and you as Captain/your teams Captain is ready to register the team, click the "Create Team" button below! The Captain should fill out the form, electing a Vice-Captain, providing team details and a logo, and listing a roster of names. Once the form has been submitted each other roster member must accept the team request via the "Manage Team Invites" button. Once all 5 starting members have accepted the team will be formed, and your first game day will be between 7 and 14 days from then.

Unregistering as a Free Agent

If you wish to unregister as a Free Agent you can do so by clicking the button below and following the steps to confirm. This will remove you from the free agents database. You can re-register at a later date if you so desire.

Leaving your Team/Unregistering as a Player

If you wish to leave your team, your team Captain and Vice-Captain must swap you to a substitute role, where will be able to leave via the "Leave Team" button and instructions below. When you leave a team your stats will be retained and you will automatically be placed in the free agents database. This must be done before you can unregister as a player.

Unbiased 3rd Party Appeal

Our staff are all volunteers who love the idea of what CompeteLeague provides. As such we don't want to deprive them of the experiece: we let staff who aren't a part of the operation of your league participate. The button below is specifically for the purpose of disputing a ruling where a member of staff was on one side or another and you feel this lead to an unjust decision. Click the button then describe the ruling and your reasoning - and CompeteLeague admins will arrange for an unbiased third party to judge the case and give a ruling. Please use appeals wisely: in allowing our staff to compete we often hold them to the rules even more closely than non-staff competitors, so make sure to check the rules and consider both sides of the ruling.
